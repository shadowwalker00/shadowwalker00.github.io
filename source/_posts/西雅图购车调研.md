# 概念
1. 4cyl什么意思: 4  cynlinder engine，4缸
2. combined fuel economy: 燃油经济性，等价于耗油量，单位是MPG(每加仑英里数)
3. Horsepower: 马力，单位是HP，a unit of measurement of power
